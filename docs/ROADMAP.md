# Roadmap

Roadmap ini berisi arah pengembangan AI UMKM Co-Pilot setelah rilis MVP v1.0.0.

## v1.0.0 — Premium MVP

Status: Completed

Scope:

- Authentication
- Business profile onboarding
- Transactions
- Products
- Stock management
- Profit report
- Rule-based AI Assistant
- Example data
- Data cleanup controls
- Premium UI
- Vercel deployment

## v1.1.0 — Product Polish

Focus: meningkatkan pengalaman pengguna harian.

Planned:

- Persistent settings preferences
- Improved empty states
- Better mobile layout polish
- More transaction filters
- Better product search
- Better report date picker
- More helpful assistant prompt examples

## v1.2.0 — Export and Reporting

Focus: laporan yang bisa dibagikan dan diarsipkan.

Planned:

- Export report to PDF
- Export transactions to CSV
- Monthly report summary
- Expense category trend
- Product margin report
- Stock movement report

## v1.3.0 — Business Targets

Focus: target usaha yang dapat disesuaikan user.

Planned:

- Daily revenue target
- Monthly profit target
- Low stock alert settings
- Dashboard target progress
- Business health indicator based on user-defined targets

## v1.4.0 — AI Assistant Upgrade

Focus: assistant yang lebih cerdas dan kontekstual.

Planned:

- LLM integration
- Better natural language understanding
- Suggested actions
- Business diagnosis
- Inventory recommendations
- Expense reduction suggestions
- Monthly business summary

## v1.5.0 — Multi Business and Team

Focus: mendukung skenario usaha yang lebih kompleks.

Planned:

- Multiple businesses per account
- Team members
- Role-based access
- Cashier role
- Owner role
- Audit log

## Long Term

- Mobile app
- Offline-first transaction input
- WhatsApp report summary
- Receipt scanning
- Marketplace integration
- POS integration
